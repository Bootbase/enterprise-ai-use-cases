## Source Quality Notes

The evidence base draws on multiple named enterprise deployments (Walmart, Maersk, SUEZ) across industries, which is a strength for cross-industry applicability. However, the majority of specific, quantified metrics come from Walmart's GNFR program, and all deployments use a single vendor platform (Pactum AI). Sources S1 and S2 are industry articles reporting on Walmart's published pilot and expansion results with named metrics. S3 is a first-party SUEZ customer story published on the SUEZ UK website. S4 through S8 and S10 are Pactum's own product pages and press releases — these are vendor claims, but they name specific clients and metrics. S9 is an industry article referencing Maersk's deployment. No independent third-party audit of the AI negotiation accuracy or savings claims has been published. The Thunderbird/ASU case study on Pactum (Walmart and Maersk) exists as a business school case but is behind a paywall and could not be independently verified for specific metrics.
